A 12-lesson interactive course · in two parts

Amelia’s Graphs Adventure 🗺️

Twelve short quests. Part 1 makes straight-line graphs your topic; Part 2 takes on the curves — inverse, parabolas, and exponentials. Each quest gives you a win you keep forever.

How this works

  • Do the lessons in order — each builds on the one before.
  • Each takes about 10–15 minutes: a bit of playing, then quick-fire drills with instant feedback.
  • Drills deal fresh questions every replay — chase the 🌟🌟🌟 and your 🔥 streaks.
  • Come back and replay drills tomorrow and next week. That’s not busywork — spacing practice out is literally how memory works.
  • Stuck on anything? Ask Claude (your tutor) — no question is too small.
